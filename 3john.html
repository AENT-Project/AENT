3 John

3 John — (AENT) Accessible English New Testament

This version is written for everyone. We use familiar names like "Jesus" and "God" rather than the original Aramaic or Hebrew names because our goal is simple — make the Bible easy to read and understand.

A quick note: Whenever you see THE LORD in all capitals, it refers to the personal, sacred name of God. When you see Lord in normal letters, it is a title of respect, similar to calling someone "Sir" or "Master."

This text is meant as a companion to your current favorite version of the New Testament — not a replacement. Nourish your soul in the Word, every way and every chance you get.

Grace & Peace.

Walk in Truth

From the Elder — to my dear friend Gaius whom I love in the truth.

Dear friend — I pray that you are doing well in every way and that you are healthy in body just as you are healthy in soul. I was so glad when some brothers came and told me about your faithfulness to the truth and how you continue to walk in it. Nothing gives me greater joy than to hear that my children are walking in the truth.

Support Those Who Go Out for the Name

Dear friend — you are being faithful in whatever you do for the brothers even when they are strangers to you. They have told the church about your love. You will do well to send them on their way in a manner worthy of God. After all they went out for the sake of the Name accepting nothing from the Gentiles — so we ought to support people like this so that we can work together for the truth.

The Problem of Diotrephes

I wrote something to the church but Diotrephes — who loves to put himself first — does not recognize our authority. So if I come I will bring up what he is doing — spreading wicked nonsense about us. And not satisfied with that he refuses to welcome the brothers himself, stops those who want to do so, and even throws them out of the church.

Imitate What Is Good

Dear friend — do not imitate what is evil but what is good. Whoever does good belongs to God — whoever does evil has not seen God. Everyone speaks well of Demetrius and the truth itself backs them up. We also speak well of him and you know that what we say is true.

Closing

I had many things to write to you but I would rather not write them with pen and ink. I hope to see you soon and we will talk face to face.

Peace be with you. Your friends here send their greetings. Greet our friends there by name.
